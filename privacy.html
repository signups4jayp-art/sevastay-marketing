Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026  ·  Sevastay LLC

This Privacy Policy describes how Sevastay LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our hotel management platform at app.sevastay.com (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and role (e.g., owner, manager). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Property and financial data: We collect information you enter about your properties, including revenue figures, expense records, ownership structure, and imported night audit reports. This data belongs to you.

Usage data: We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including pages visited, features used, and actions taken, to improve the platform.

Device and technical data: We automatically collect IP address, browser type, device type, and timestamps of access for security and diagnostic purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain account security
  • To process your subscription and send billing-related communications
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To send product updates and important service notices (you may opt out of marketing emails)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents

3. AI Features

Sevastay uses AI (powered by Anthropic Claude) to analyze uploaded documents and generate insights such as owner narratives and night audit summaries. Document content is sent to Anthropic's API solely to perform this analysis. We do not use your data to train AI models. Anthropic's data handling practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers: Infrastructure and hosting (Supabase, Vercel), email delivery (Resend), and AI processing (Anthropic) — all under data processing agreements
  • Co-owners you authorize: If you grant access to other users on your account, they can view data according to their assigned role
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the safety of users or the public

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and property data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel, we retain your data for 90 days before deletion, giving you time to export. Demo accounts are automatically deleted after 14–30 days of inactivity.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage, row-level access controls, and multi-factor authentication. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at admin@sevastay.com.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@sevastay.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and authentication state. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Your browser may also receive cookies from third-party services we embed (e.g., authentication infrastructure).

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or a prominent notice in the Service at least 14 days before they take effect.

11. Contact

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at admin@sevastay.com.
